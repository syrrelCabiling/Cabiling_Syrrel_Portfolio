The basis of this assignment is to create an interactive application using 3D, video, and still images. My team and I designed and developed an engaging experience for the end user with which they can interact in a one-page mobile-first web application.

As the Graphic Designer of the team, I was responsible for designing the user experience and interface of the app where users can modify the thermostat, whether they are at home or away.

To formalize our brand, we needed a logo. Some of the keywords we all agreed to are safe, secure, temperature, comfort, and home. This is the the final logo I presented to them. My idea was to incorporate a house, lock, and sun all in one logo. These elements captured everything that the ThermoSecure by HomeCom system has to offer; those being security, temperature control, weather and air monitoring, all for your home.

Finally, I designed a corresponding sell-sheet to entice the public with this new product.

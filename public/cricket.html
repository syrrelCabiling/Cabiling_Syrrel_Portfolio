CRICKET 101

Our class was tasked to research and design an infographic that would explain the basics of Cricket (the sport). As someone who had no idea how to play Cricket at all, I turn to research. My main goal is to simplify the information I found and relay that to viewers. This is my version to Cricket 101. The design approach I did is to make it appealing as possible so that it would not bore someone who is looking at it and to avoid information overload.
